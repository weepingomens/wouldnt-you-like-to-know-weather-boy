hi folks! i’d like to write a heist, or die trying.

this heist will outwardly focus on two things:

(a) the retrieval of a hathorne relic from the SL museum of the occult. details surrounding the object are hazy, but if legends are to be believed, possession of this relic allows⁠ werewolves greater power over their lupus and transformations. such power comes at a price, however⁠—and at present, possession or destruction of the artifact may be the only way to ensure wren stern’s future.

(b) the infiltration of the leviathan society records room within the base underneath the museum which contains all of the information collected on those formerly imprisoned in solomon, before the recent breakout, and the destruction of the contents within it⁠—in order to free the former prisoners in mind and soul as well as in body.

sounds simple enough.

right?
narratively speaking, heist will center on red tail pack, so while a majority of the heist players should probably be werewolves, there’s no reason why all of them need to be, as long as you can devise a coherent reason on why they’d be involved in red tail affairs! (but for consistency’s sake, i’ll cap out humans / witches / vampires involved at one per group, all of which are still available).

i want to finish heist before the site timeskip (i.e. before or during mid-june) and that deadline is non-negotiable, so to keep momentum going, starting in may, i intend to NPC (or app) the starred roles (★) listed below if an application has not been submitted for them, to give myself authorial breathing room with meeting the deadline. starting in june, i intend to swap over to the “solo only” portion of heist⁠ proper—unfucking any any all plot threads, working towards a neat (or neat-ish) conclusion for jack’s arc, and overall devising a way to get jack to the hazy ending for this plotline i envision for her. i’ll do my best to communicate these deadlines again as they come closer, but it’s something to keep in mind!

if you have questions / comments / concerns / ideas / want to get involved / want more details, let me know in DMs or via a ping in the site server, and let’s go out with a bang!
