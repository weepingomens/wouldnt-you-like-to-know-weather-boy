guidelines

Welcome to Weeping Omens' Site Store! Here you may be able to utilize the points you have acquired throughout your stay in the site to purchase a variety of things. These can all range from additional character slots, member services, special features, reservations, or even limited promotions and opportunities. On occasion, points may also be used to participate in exclusive events within the site.

These points may be earned through several methods: REGISTERING AN ACCOUNT, POSTING, TRADING, OR WITHDRAWING FROM THE DONATION POT. Members are free to do as they'd like with the points they accumulate as well. Just think of them as in-site currency. So long as you either inform staff or post in this thread what you intend to do with your points, we can easily get to it!
